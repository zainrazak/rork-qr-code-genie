Frequently Asked Questions

How many QR codes can I create for free?

You can create up to 5 QR codes for free. After that, upgrade to QR Code Genie Pro for unlimited creation.

What types of QR codes can I create?

You can create QR codes for URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, and Wi-Fi network credentials.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find QR Code Genie and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open QR Code Genie, go to the Upgrade screen, and tap "Restore Purchase" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID.

Is my data stored online?

No. All QR codes and scan history are stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any personal data.

Subscription Terms

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Account Settings on the App Store

Privacy Policy

QR Code Genie respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All QR codes and scan history are stored locally on your device and never leave it. We do not use analytics or tracking tools. The only network communication is with Apple's App Store for subscription verification.
